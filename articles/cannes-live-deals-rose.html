The Rosé Index: Measuring Activation ROI

Not all Cannes activations are created equal. The Rosé Index is The Upside Journal's proprietary (and slightly tongue-in-cheek) framework for measuring whether an activation generated business value or just social media content.

How it works: We track three metrics for each major activation:

1. Meeting density: How many structured 1:1 or small-group meetings happened at the venue? 2. Follow-up velocity: How quickly did attendees send follow-up emails after the event? 3. Deal attribution: Can any closed or in-progress deal be traced to an introduction at this activation?
