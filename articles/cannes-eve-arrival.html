What Smart Operators Do the Night Before

1. Map Your First 48 Hours

The founders and executives who extract real value from Cannes don't freestyle their schedule. By the time they arrive, they have:

  • Confirmed meetings for Days 1 and 2 (the highest-energy days)
  • Identified three "must-attend" evening events where decision-makers will be relaxed and accessible
  • Briefed their team on who to approach and what to say using tools like ConcordeApp's relationship mapping
