The 21-Day Window

Historical data from previous Cannes festivals shows that 78% of deals initiated at the event either advance to contract stage or die within 21 days of the festival closing.

The implication: if your Cannes meetings haven't produced a signed term sheet or formal proposal by July 17, they probably won't.

ConcordeApp's data adds nuance: deals where both parties had pre-Cannes contact (even a single email) close at 2.3× the rate of cold introductions made at the festival.
