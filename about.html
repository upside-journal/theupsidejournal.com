Chaste Inegbedion

Editor-in-Chief · Upside Journal

Chaste Inegbedion leads the editorial vision of Upside Journal — the relaunched legacy publication that Silicon Valley insiders read from 1989 to 2002, now reborn for a new era of technology, capital, and power.

As Editor-in-Chief, Chaste drives the publication's coverage across eight verticals — from enterprise AI infrastructure and $300 billion venture quarters to African sovereignty and the policy decisions shaping the global tech economy. His writing sits at the intersection of deep technical analysis and the human stories behind the numbers.

He is also the founder of The Pitch Journey, where he works with founders navigating the fundraising landscape. His perspective bridges the gap between builders who ship and the capital markets that fund them.

Olusegun (Olu) Olufunmiloye

Publisher · Upside Journal

Olusegun (Olu) Olufunmiloye is the Publisher of Upside Journal — the driving force behind the publication's business strategy, partnerships, and its mission to become the intelligence platform that C-suite executives, venture capitalists, and policymakers trust to cut through the noise.

A serial entrepreneur and technologist, Olu is the founder of CNE Concepts Ltd, CNE Studios, and Cocoa Soft Africa — ventures spanning digital media production, creative technology, and African software infrastructure. His work sits at the convergence of AI-driven business transformation and emerging market opportunity.

At Upside Journal, Olu writes thought pieces focused on using AI in 2026 for business — not the theory, but the operational reality of how companies deploy artificial intelligence to build competitive advantage. He brings the operator's perspective: what works, what doesn't, and what the pitch decks won't tell you.
